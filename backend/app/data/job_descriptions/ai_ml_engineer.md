# AI/ML Engineer — ABC Company (Sample JD)

We're looking for an AI/ML Engineer to build and optimize our production
NLP and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems, including our
multilingual customer support chatbot.

## Responsibilities
- Design and optimize RAG pipelines (retrieval, chunking, re-ranking, generation).
- Fine-tune and evaluate LLMs for multilingual support.
- Collaborate with product and engineering to ship AI features to production.
- Write clean, well-tested Python code.

## Requirements
- Strong Python skills; experience with data structures & algorithms.
- Experience with LLM APIs, embeddings, and vector search.
- Familiarity with prompt engineering and evaluation techniques.
- Good communication skills and ability to explain technical tradeoffs.

## Nice to have
- Experience with multilingual NLP systems.
- Experience with FastAPI or similar backend frameworks.
